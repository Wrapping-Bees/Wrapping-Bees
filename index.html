Wrapping bees


Introduction

Our product, Wrapping Bees, is an innovative way to preserve food in a fresh condition for long periods of time. Wrapping Bees focuses on SDG #12 Responsible Consumption and Production, as it reduces the overproduction of plastics and aluminum in an eco-friendly and reusable way.

"Wax on, waste off"

Read More

Description

Its objective is to reduce the mayor amount of waste generated by human consumption and production. Wrapping bees is a great alternative to plastic wraps, aluminum foils, and Ziploc bags, which take hundreds of years to decompose. Our reusable wraps are sustainable, biodegradable and reusable for up to a year, meeting the needs of responsible consumption.

Materials

Wrapping Bees is made of 100% cotton fabric sheets, beeswax and coconut oil. This materials give our product special properties like freshness, as well as protecting your leftovers from moisture, air and bacterias.

Benefits

Our product will grant you a reusable and year-long beeswax wrap, that is not only a great food preservative but also a sustainable and eco-friendly solution that'll contribute to reaching SDG #12.
